Become a Sponsor

Student Robotics is looking for new sponsors to help us fulfil our mission. The support from our sponsors will make it possible to sustain our programme and continue to run free to enter competitions that attract students from a wide range of schools and colleges. By partnering with us, you will be able to increase your organisation or product awareness with the next generation of engineers.

Every year it costs us more than £20,000 to run the competition, and we simply cannot do this without the help of sponsors.

Sponsor Levels

Bronze

£500 or more

  • Link and logo on our website
  • Company name, logo and brief overview in our competition booklet
  • Invite for a representative to attend the competition

Silver

£1,000 or more

  • All the perks of Bronze sponsorship
  • Friendly mention on social media
  • Logo on a banner at the competition
  • An opportunity during the year to present your organisation to our student competitors

Gold

£2,000 or more

  • All the perks of Silver sponsorship
  • Logo on the volunteer t-shirts
  • Friendly mention and thank you at the competition

Platinum

£10,000 or more

  • All the perks of Gold sponsorship
  • Dedicated space at the competition
  • Set a challenge for teams, and declare the winner at the competition**
  • Short (~5 min.) pre-recorded presentation at the start of the competition, to talk about your company and set your challenge, and a short in-person presentation on the final weekend to announce the winner

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can sponsor?

Any individual or organisation is able to sponsor Student Robotics.

Why do we need sponsors

We do not charge the teams in order to enter the competition. However there are still costs associated in running the competition , and as such we depend upon commercial sponsors in order to keep running. Additionally, the robotics kits that are loaned to teams require ongoing maintenance and repairs. Thanks to our sponsors we are able to continue to offer this at no cost to the competitors.

Can I add Gift Aid to my donation?

If you are a UK tax payer and eligible to give Gift Aid then you can add Gift Aid to your donation. Please donate through the JustGiving link above and tick the Gift Aid box.

How does sponsoring an award work?

Each year we present 5 awards to teams based on their involvement throughout the year, and not based on the competition winners. Details of these awards can be found in the current rulebook. By sponsoring one of these awards we will add your company name and logo onto to trophy, and announce your company name when presenting the award.

If you wish to suggest a new award, please e-mail us using the form below and we can discuss options.

How can I donate?

There are 2 options for donating;

1) you can use the JustGiving link above. This will also allow you to add Gift Aid. If you wish to be recognised as a Bronze, Silver, Gold, or Platinum sponsor please also contact us using the above form so we can easily identify your donation.

2) contact us using the above form and we can provide details for a bank transfer.

How much can I sponsor?

Any amount of sponsorship is welcome! No matter how small, several small donations from multiple donors do add up and can make a big difference.

However, unless you exceed the £500 for Bronze level sponsorship we are unable to do anything to directly recognise your donation.

How does setting a challenge work?

You record a 5 minute video introducing yourself and describing the challenge, and provide a written description to be included in the rulebook. The video and rules will be released at the Kickstart event at the beginning of the competition and available online throughout the year.

At the end of the competition the winner will be decided and announced. A representative of your company will be invited to help decide the winners and to present the award. The winner will ultimately be decided by Student Robotics, guided by your representative's input.
